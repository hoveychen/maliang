Tracks (source MP3 from incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/,
resampled to 32kHz stereo 16-bit PCM WAV with leading/trailing silence trimmed):

  bgm_carefree.wav       <- "Carefree"            (~204s)  ukulele + whistle, sunny acoustic
  bgm_cheery_monday.wav  <- "Cheery Monday"       (~78s)   bright playful
  bgm_happy_boy.wav      <- "Happy Boy End Theme" (~52s)   warm closing theme

game_audio.gd plays these three in rotation: each track plays through in full,
then crossfades (CROSSFADE_SECS) to the next — one full cycle is ~5m34s before
any track repeats. bgm_carefree.wav is BGM_STEPS[0] and also beds the main menu.

Previously this folder shipped bgm_main.wav ("Children's Game Music 2 - Adventure"
by heartade, CC0, opengameart.org) — a single ~68.5s loop. It was replaced because
a 68s loop felt too repetitive and too electronic in tone. Its predecessor
bgm_step1/2/3.wav ("Music Loop Variations" by "obscure music", CC0) were 9.6s
intensity layers cycled every ~19s, which sounded choppy and never settled.
