File bgm_main.wav is the "Adventure" track from the "Children's Game Music"
series (source children_soundtrack_2.wav, 44.1kHz float stereo), resampled to
32kHz stereo 16-bit PCM with leading/trailing silence trimmed to ~68.5s for a
tight seamless loop.

Previously this folder shipped bgm_step1/2/3.wav ("Music Loop Variations" by
"obscure music", also CC0, https://opengameart.org/content/music-loop-variations).
Those three 9.6s intensity layers were replaced by this single continuous track:
cycling the short layers every ~19s sounded choppy and never settled.
